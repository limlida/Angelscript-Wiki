### FSubmixEffectEQBand


A multiband EQ submix effect.

**属性**:

- `float32 Bandwidth [The bandwidth, in octaves, of the EQ band]`
- `float32 Frequency [Cutoff frequency (in hz) of the EQ band]`
- `float32 GainDb [The gain in decibels of the EQ band]`
- `bool bEnabled [Whether or not the EQ band is enabled]`


**方法**:

- `FSubmixEffectEQBand& opAssign(FSubmixEffectEQBand Other)`
