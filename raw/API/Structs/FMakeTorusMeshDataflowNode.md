### FMakeTorusMeshDataflowNode


Make a torus mesh

**属性**:

- `FVector Origin [Torus will be translated so that center is at this point]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `int ProfileVertexCount [Number of vertices on the profile]`
- `float32 Radius1 [Radius of the profile]`
- `float32 Radius2 [Radius of sweep curve]`
- `int SweepVertexCount [Number of vertices on the sweep curve]`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FMakeTorusMeshDataflowNode& opAssign(FMakeTorusMeshDataflowNode Other)`
