### FItemWithQuantity


Just a nice way of defining an item along with some amount of the item

**属性**:

- `TSoftClassPtr<UNarrativeItem> Item`
- `int Quantity`
- `bool bRefreshDaily [是否每日刷新（目前仅对ItemCollectionsToGrant有效!）]`


**方法**:

- `FItemWithQuantity& opAssign(FItemWithQuantity Other)`
