### FFOscillator


Defines oscillation of a single number.

**属性**:

- `float32 Amplitude [Amplitude of the sinusoidal oscillation.]`
- `float32 Frequency [Frequency of the sinusoidal oscillation.]`
- `EInitialOscillatorOffset InitialOffset [Defines how to begin (either at zero, or at a randomized value.]`
- `EOscillatorWaveform Waveform [Type of waveform to use for oscillation.]`


**方法**:

- `FFOscillator& opAssign(FFOscillator Other)`
