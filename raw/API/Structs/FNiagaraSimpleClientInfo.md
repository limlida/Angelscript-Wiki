### FNiagaraSimpleClientInfo


Simple information on the connected client for use in continuous or immediate response UI elements.

**属性**:

- `TArray<FString> Actors [List of all actors with Niagara components.]`
- `TArray<FString> Components [List of all Niagara components.]`
- `TArray<FString> Emitters [List of all Niagara emitters.]`
- `TArray<FString> Systems [List of all system names in the scene.]`


**方法**:

- `FNiagaraSimpleClientInfo& opAssign(FNiagaraSimpleClientInfo Other)`
