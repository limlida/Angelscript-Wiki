### FCameraActorAttachmentInfo


Attachment information for a camera rig.

**属性**:

- `AActor Actor [The actor to attach to.]`
- `FName BoneName [An optional bone to attach to on the actor.]`
- `FName SocketName [An optional socket to attach to on the actor.]`
- `float32 Weight [The weight of this attachment. Unused if only one attachment is used.]`


**方法**:

- `FCameraActorAttachmentInfo& opAssign(FCameraActorAttachmentInfo Other)`
