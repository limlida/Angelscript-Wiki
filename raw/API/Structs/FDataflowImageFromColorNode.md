### FDataflowImageFromColorNode


Create a RGBA image from a single color at a specific resolution
Outputs are single channel images

**属性**:

- `FLinearColor FillColor [color  to fill the image with]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `EDataflowImageResolution Resolution [resolution of the output image]`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FDataflowImageFromColorNode& opAssign(FDataflowImageFromColorNode Other)`
