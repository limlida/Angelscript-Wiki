### FAudioMaterialEnvelopeStyle


Represents the appearance of an Audio Material Envelope

**属性**:

- `FLinearColor BackgroundColor`
- `FLinearColor CurveColor`
- `FVector2f DesiredSize [Desired Draw size of the rendered material]`
- `UMaterialInterface Material [Material used to render the Slate]`
- `FLinearColor OutlineColor`


**方法**:

- `FAudioMaterialEnvelopeStyle& opAssign(FAudioMaterialEnvelopeStyle Other)`
