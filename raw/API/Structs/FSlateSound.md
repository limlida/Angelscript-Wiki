### FSlateSound


An intermediary to make UBaseSound available for Slate to play sounds

**属性**:

- `UObject ResourceObject [Pointer to the USoundBase. Holding onto it as a UObject because USoundBase is not available in Slate core.
Edited via FSlateSoundStructCustomization to ensure you can only set USoundBase assets on it.]`


**方法**:

- `FSlateSound& opAssign(FSlateSound Other)`
