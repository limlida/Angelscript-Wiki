### FGroomAssetToCollectionDataflowNode


Transform a groom asset to a collection

**属性**:

- `float32 CurvesThickness [Curves thickness for geometry generation]`
- `EGroomCollectionType CurvesType [Type of curves to use to fill the groom collection (guides/strands)]`
- `const UGroomAsset GroomAsset [Input asset to read the guides from]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FGroomAssetToCollectionDataflowNode& opAssign(FGroomAssetToCollectionDataflowNode Other)`
