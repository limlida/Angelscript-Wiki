### FPassiveSoundMixModifier


Structure containing information on a SoundMix to activate passively.

**属性**:

- `float32 MaxVolumeThreshold [Maximum volume level required to activate SoundMix. Above this value the SoundMix will not be active.]`
- `float32 MinVolumeThreshold [Minimum volume level required to activate SoundMix. Below this value the SoundMix will not be active.]`
- `USoundMix SoundMix [The SoundMix to activate]`


**方法**:

- `FPassiveSoundMixModifier& opAssign(FPassiveSoundMixModifier Other)`
