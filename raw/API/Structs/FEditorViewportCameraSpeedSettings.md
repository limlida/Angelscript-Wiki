### FEditorViewportCameraSpeedSettings


**属性**:

- `float32 AbsoluteMaxSpeed [The fastest possible speed of the  camera]`
- `float32 AbsoluteMinSpeed [The slowest possible speed of the camera]`
- `float32 MaxUISpeed [The maximum speed of the camera when dragging with a slider]`
- `float32 MinUISpeed [The minimum speed of the camera when dragging with a slider]`


**方法**:

- `FEditorViewportCameraSpeedSettings& opAssign(FEditorViewportCameraSpeedSettings Other)`
