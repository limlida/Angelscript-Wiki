### FCameraShakePatternStartParams


Parameters for starting a camera shake pattern.

**属性**:

- `float32 DurationOverride [An optional override for the camera shake's duration]`
- `bool bIsRestarting [Whether the camera shake is restarting while playing]`
- `bool bOverrideDuration [Whether the camera shake's duration is overriden (see DurationOverride)]`


**方法**:

- `FCameraShakePatternStartParams& opAssign(FCameraShakePatternStartParams Other)`
