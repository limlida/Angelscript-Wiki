### FHairSimulationSettings


**属性**:

- `FHairSimulationForces ExternalForces [External Forces for the hair physics]`
- `FHairSimulationConstraints MaterialConstraints [Material Constraints for the hair physics]`
- `FHairSimulationSetup SimulationSetup [Solver Settings for the hair physics]`
- `FHairSimulationSolver SolverSettings [Solver Settings for the hair physics]`
- `bool bOverrideSettings [Boolean to control if we are going to override the groom asset physics settings]`


**方法**:

- `FHairSimulationSettings& opAssign(FHairSimulationSettings Other)`
