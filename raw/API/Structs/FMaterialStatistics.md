### FMaterialStatistics


**属性**:

- `int NumInterpolatorScalars [Number of interpolator scalars required for user-defined interpolators]`
- `int NumPixelShaderInstructions [Number of instructions used by most expensive pixel shader in the material]`
- `int NumPixelTextureSamples [Number of textures sampled by the pixel shader]`
- `int NumSamplers [Number of samplers required by the material]`
- `int NumUVScalars [Number of interpolator scalars required for UVs]`
- `int NumVertexShaderInstructions [Number of instructions used by most expensive vertex shader in the material]`
- `int NumVertexTextureSamples [Number of textures sampled by the vertex shader]`
- `int NumVirtualTextureSamples [Number of virtual textures sampled]`


**方法**:

- `FMaterialStatistics& opAssign(FMaterialStatistics Other)`
