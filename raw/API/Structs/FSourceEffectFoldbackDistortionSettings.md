### FSourceEffectFoldbackDistortionSettings


**属性**:

- `float32 InputGainDb [The amount of gain to add to input to allow forcing the triggering of the threshold]`
- `float32 OutputGainDb [The amount of gain to apply to the output]`
- `float32 ThresholdDb [If the audio amplitude is higher than this, it will fold back]`


**方法**:

- `FSourceEffectFoldbackDistortionSettings& opAssign(FSourceEffectFoldbackDistortionSettings Other)`
