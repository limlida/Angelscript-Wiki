### FAddCustomCollectionAttributeDataflowNode


Adds custom attribute to Collection

**属性**:

- `FString AttrName [Attribute name]`
- `ECustomAttributeTypeEnum CustomAttributeType [Attribute type]`
- `FString CustomGroupName [User specified group name]`
- `EStandardGroupNameEnum GroupName [Standard group names]`
- `int NumElements [Number of elements for the attribute]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FAddCustomCollectionAttributeDataflowNode& opAssign(FAddCustomCollectionAttributeDataflowNode Other)`
