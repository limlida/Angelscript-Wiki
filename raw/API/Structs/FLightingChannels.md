### FLightingChannels


Specifies which lighting channels are relevant

**属性**:

- `bool bChannel0 [Default channel for all primitives and lights.]`
- `bool bChannel1 [First custom channel]`
- `bool bChannel2 [Second custom channel]`


**方法**:

- `FLightingChannels& opAssign(FLightingChannels Other)`
