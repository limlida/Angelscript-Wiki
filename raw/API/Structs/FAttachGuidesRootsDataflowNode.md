### FAttachGuidesRootsDataflowNode


Attach the guides roots by setting their kinematic weights to 1.0f

**属性**:

- `int GroupIndex [Group index on which the roots will be attached. -1 will attach all the groups]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FAttachGuidesRootsDataflowNode& opAssign(FAttachGuidesRootsDataflowNode Other)`
