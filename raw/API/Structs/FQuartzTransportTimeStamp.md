### FQuartzTransportTimeStamp


Transport Time stamp, used for tracking the musical time stamp on a clock

**属性**:

- `int Bars [The current bar this clock is on]`
- `int Beat [The current beat this clock is on]`
- `float32 BeatFraction [A fractional representation of the time that's played since the last bear]`
- `float32 Seconds [The time in seconds that this TimeStamp occured at]`


**方法**:

- `FQuartzTransportTimeStamp& opAssign(FQuartzTransportTimeStamp Other)`
