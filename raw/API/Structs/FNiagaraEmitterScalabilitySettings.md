### FNiagaraEmitterScalabilitySettings


Scalability settings for Niagara Emitters on a particular platform set.

**属性**:

- `FNiagaraPlatformSet Platforms [The platforms on which these settings are active (unless overridden).]`
- `float32 SpawnCountScale [Scale factor applied to spawn counts for this emitter.]`
- `bool bScaleSpawnCount [Enable spawn count scaling]`


**方法**:

- `FNiagaraEmitterScalabilitySettings& opAssign(FNiagaraEmitterScalabilitySettings Other)`
