### FViewLightingChannels


View / light masking support

**属性**:

- `bool bViewChannel0 [View specific lighting channel 0 (enabled by default).]`
- `bool bViewChannel1 [View specific lighting channel 1.]`
- `bool bViewChannel2 [View specific lighting channel 2.]`
- `bool bViewChannel3 [View specific lighting channel 3.]`
- `bool bViewChannel4 [View specific lighting channel 4.]`


**方法**:

- `FViewLightingChannels& opAssign(FViewLightingChannels Other)`
