### FCameraLensSettings


#note, this struct has a details customization in CameraLensSettingsCustomization.cpp/h

**属性**:

- `int DiaphragmBladeCount [Number of blades of diaphragm.]`
- `float32 MaxFStop [Maximum aperture for this lens (e.g. 2.8 for an f/2.8 lens)]`
- `float32 MaxFocalLength [Maximum focal length for this lens]`
- `float32 MinFStop [Minimum aperture for this lens (e.g. 2.8 for an f/2.8 lens)]`
- `float32 MinFocalLength [Minimum focal length for this lens]`
- `float32 MinimumFocusDistance [Shortest distance this lens can focus on.]`
- `float32 SqueezeFactor [Squeeze factor for anamorphic lenses.]`


**方法**:

- `FCameraLensSettings& opAssign(FCameraLensSettings Other)`
