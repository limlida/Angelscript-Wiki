### FPerlinLayerProperties


Per-layer properties for Perlin noise. Each layer has independent Frequency and Intensity.

**属性**:

- `float32 Frequency [Frequency of Perlin noise layer]`
- `float32 Intensity [Intensity/amplitude of Perlin noise layer]`


**方法**:

- `FPerlinLayerProperties& opAssign(FPerlinLayerProperties Other)`
