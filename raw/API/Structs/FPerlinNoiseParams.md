### FPerlinNoiseParams


Component data for Perlin Noise channels

**属性**:

- `float Amplitude [The amplitude of the noise, which will vary between [-Amplitude, +Amplitude]]`
- `float32 Frequency [The frequency of the noise, i.e. how many times per second does the noise peak]`
- `float32 Offset [Starting offset, in seconds, into the noise pattern]`


**方法**:

- `FPerlinNoiseParams& opAssign(FPerlinNoiseParams Other)`
