### FExplodedViewDataflowNode


"Explodes" the pieces from the Collection for better visualization

**属性**:

- `FVector Offset [Translate collection for exploded view]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `FVector Scale [Scale amounts to expand the pieces in all 3 directions]`
- `float32 UniformScale [Scale amount to expand the pieces uniformly in all directions]`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FExplodedViewDataflowNode& opAssign(FExplodedViewDataflowNode Other)`
