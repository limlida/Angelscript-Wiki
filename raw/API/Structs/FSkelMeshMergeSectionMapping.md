### FSkelMeshMergeSectionMapping


Info to map all the sections from a single source skeletal mesh to
a final section entry in the merged skeletal mesh

**属性**:

- `TArray<int> SectionIDs [indices to final section entries of the merged skel mesh]`


**方法**:

- `FSkelMeshMergeSectionMapping& opAssign(FSkelMeshMergeSectionMapping Other)`
