### FTViewTarget


A ViewTarget is the primary actor the camera is associated with.

**属性**:

- `FMinimalViewInfo POV [Computed point of view]`
- `APlayerState PlayerState [PlayerState (used to follow same player through pawn transitions, etc., when spectating)]`
- `AActor Target [Target Actor used to compute POV]`


**方法**:

- `FTViewTarget& opAssign(FTViewTarget Other)`
