### FCollectionTransformSelectionCustomDataflowNode_v2


Selects specified bones in the GeometryCollection by using a
comma separated list, e.g. "0, 2, 5-10, 12-15"

- `FString BoneIndices [Comma separated list of single or a range of bone indices to specify the selection, e.g. "0, 2, 5-10, 12-15"]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`
