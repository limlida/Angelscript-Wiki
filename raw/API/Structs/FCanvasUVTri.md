### FCanvasUVTri


Simple 2d triangle with UVs

**属性**:

- `FLinearColor V0_Color [Color of first vertex]`
- `FVector2D V0_Pos [Position of first vertex]`
- `FVector2D V0_UV [UV of first vertex]`
- `FLinearColor V1_Color [Color of second vertex]`
- `FVector2D V1_Pos [Position of second vertex]`
- `FVector2D V1_UV [UV of second vertex]`
- `FLinearColor V2_Color [Color of third vertex]`
- `FVector2D V2_Pos [Position of third vertex]`
- `FVector2D V2_UV [UV of third vertex]`


**方法**:

- `FCanvasUVTri& opAssign(FCanvasUVTri Other)`
