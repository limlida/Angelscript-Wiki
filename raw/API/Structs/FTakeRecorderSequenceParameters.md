### FTakeRecorderSequenceParameters


Parameters for initializing a new sequence.

**属性**:

- `UTakePreset BasePreset [A preset asset to base the recording off]`
- `ULevelSequence BaseSequence [A level sequence asset to base the recording off]`
- `ULevelSequence RecordIntoSequence [A level sequence asset to record into]`
- `ULevelSequence SequenceToView [A sequence that should be shown directly on the take recorder UI]`


**方法**:

- `FTakeRecorderSequenceParameters& opAssign(FTakeRecorderSequenceParameters Other)`
