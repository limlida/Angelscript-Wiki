### FCollectionSelectionConvertDataflowNode


Converts Vertex/Face/Transform selection into Vertex/Face/Transform selection

**属性**:

- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bAllElementsMustBeSelected [If true then for converting vertex/face selection to transform selection all vertex/face must be selected for selecting the associated transform]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FCollectionSelectionConvertDataflowNode& opAssign(FCollectionSelectionConvertDataflowNode Other)`
