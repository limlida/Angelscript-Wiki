### FIntMargin


Describes the space around a 2D area on an integer grid.

**属性**:

- `int Bottom [Holds the margin to the bottom.]`
- `int Left [Holds the margin to the left.]`
- `int Right [Holds the margin to the right.]`
- `int Top [Holds the margin to the top.]`


**方法**:

- `FIntMargin& opAssign(FIntMargin Other)`
