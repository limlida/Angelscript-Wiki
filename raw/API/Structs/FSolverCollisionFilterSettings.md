### FSolverCollisionFilterSettings


**属性**:

- `bool FilterEnabled [Filter is enabled.]`
- `float32 MinImpulse [The minimum impulse threshold for the results.]`
- `float32 MinMass [The minimum mass threshold for the results (compared with min of particle 1 mass and particle 2 mass).]`
- `float32 MinSpeed [The min velocity threshold for the results (compared with min of particle 1 speed and particle 2 speed).]`


**方法**:

- `FSolverCollisionFilterSettings& opAssign(FSolverCollisionFilterSettings Other)`
