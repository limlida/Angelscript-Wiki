### FWaveOscillator


A wave oscillator for a single number.

**属性**:

- `float32 Amplitude [Amplitude of the sinusoidal oscillation.]`
- `float32 Frequency [Frequency of the sinusoidal oscillation.]`
- `EInitialWaveOscillatorOffsetType InitialOffsetType [Defines how to begin the oscillation.]`


**方法**:

- `FWaveOscillator& opAssign(FWaveOscillator Other)`
