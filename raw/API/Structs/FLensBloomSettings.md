### FLensBloomSettings


**属性**:

- `FConvolutionBloomSettings Convolution [Bloom convolution method specific settings.]`
- `FGaussianSumBloomSettings GaussianSum [Bloom gaussian sum method specific settings.]`
- `float32 Intensity [Multiplier for all bloom contributions >=0: off, 1(default), >1 brighter]`
- `EBloomMethod Method [Bloom algorithm]`


**方法**:

- `FLensBloomSettings& opAssign(FLensBloomSettings Other)`
