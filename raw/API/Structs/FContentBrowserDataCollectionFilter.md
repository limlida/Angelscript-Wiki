### FContentBrowserDataCollectionFilter


Data used to filter items by their collection.
@note This will typically limit your query to items that support being inside a collection.

**属性**:

- `bool bIncludeChildCollections [Whether we should include child collections in this query]`


**方法**:

- `FContentBrowserDataCollectionFilter& opAssign(FContentBrowserDataCollectionFilter Other)`
