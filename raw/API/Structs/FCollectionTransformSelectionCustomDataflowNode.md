### FCollectionTransformSelectionCustomDataflowNode


Selects specified bones in the GeometryCollection by using a
space separated list, e.g. "0 1 2 12 23"

**属性**:

- `FString BoneIndicies [Space separated list of bone indices to specify the selection, e.g. "0 1 2 3 23 34"]`
- `FLinearColor OverrideColor []`
- `bool bActive [Used to calculate ContainerElementOffsets]`
- `bool bOverrideColor []`


**方法**:

- `FCollectionTransformSelectionCustomDataflowNode& opAssign(FCollectionTransformSelectionCustomDataflowNode Other)`
