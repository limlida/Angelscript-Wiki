### UCommonVisualAttachment


Adds a widget as a zero-size attachment to another. Think icons to the left of labels, without changing the computed size of the label.

**属性**:

- `FVector2D ContentAnchor [Content Anchor Point as a ratio of the content size. Use (1.0, 1.0) to anchor the content on the bottom right, (0.0) to anchor top left, (0.5, 0.5) to anchor centered.]`
