### UCopyBonesModifier


Animation Modifier to copy the transform of 'SourceBone(s)' to 'TargetBone(s)'

**属性**:

- `TArray<FBoneReferencePair> BonePairs [Source and Target bone pairs]`
- `EAnimPoseSpaces BonePoseSpace [Space to convert SourceBone transforms into before copying them to TargetBone]`
