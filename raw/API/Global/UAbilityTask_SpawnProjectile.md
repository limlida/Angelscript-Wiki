### UAbilityTask_SpawnProjectile


Spawns a Narrative Projectile, and waits for it to generate target data. Similar to WaitTargetData, but simplified a lot.

**属性**:

- `FProjectileTargetDataDelegate OnDestroyed [Calls when projectile was destroyed.]`
- `FProjectileTargetDataDelegate OnTargetData [Ability Task listens to this to broadcast projectile hitting something.]`
