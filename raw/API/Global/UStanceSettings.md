### UStanceSettings


StanceSettings: collection of settings that are shared through the mover component and contain stance related settings.

**属性**:

- `float32 CrouchHalfHeight [New capsule half height while crouching]`
- `float32 CrouchedEyeHeight [New eye height while crouching]`
- `float32 CrouchingMaxAcceleration [New max acceleration while in crouching]`
- `float32 CrouchingMaxSpeed [New max speed while in crouching]`
