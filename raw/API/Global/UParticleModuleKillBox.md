### UParticleModuleKillBox


**属性**:

- `FRawDistributionVector LowerLeftCorner [The lower left corner of the box.]`
- `FRawDistributionVector UpperRightCorner [The upper right corner of the box.]`
- `bool bAbsolute [If true, the box coordinates are in world space./]`
- `bool bAxisAlignedAndFixedSize [If true, the box will always be axis aligned and non-scalable.]`
- `bool bKillInside [If true, particles INSIDE the box will be killed.
If false (the default), particles OUTSIDE the box will be killed.]`
