### USimpleCameraShakePattern


A base class for a simple camera shake.

**属性**:

- `float32 BlendInTime [Blend-in time for this shake. Zero or less means no blend-in.]`
- `float32 BlendOutTime [Blend-out time for this shake. Zero or less means no blend-out.]`
- `float32 Duration [Duration in seconds of this shake. Zero or less means infinite.]`
