### USoundNodeDoppler


Computes doppler pitch shift

**属性**:

- `float32 DopplerIntensity [How much to scale the doppler shift (1.0 is normal).]`
- `float32 SmoothingInterpSpeed [Speed at which to interp pitch scale]`
- `bool bUseSmoothing [Whether or not to do a smooth interp to our doppler]`
