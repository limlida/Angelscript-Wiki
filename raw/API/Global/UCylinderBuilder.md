### UCylinderBuilder


**属性**:

- `bool AlignToSide [Whether to align the brush to a face]`
- `bool Hollow [Whether this is a hollow or solid cylinder]`
- `float32 InnerRadius [Radius of inner cylinder (when hollow)]`
- `float32 OuterRadius [Radius of cylinder]`
- `int Sides [How many sides this cylinder should have]`
- `float32 Z [Distance from base to tip of cylinder]`
