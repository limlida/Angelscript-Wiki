### UNarrativeActorProvider


Provides an actor/transform on top of the transform. Similar to EQSContexts, but more general gameplay oriented.

**属性**:

- `FOnProviderActorReady OnProviderActorReady [This delegate can be used in cases where the actor to provide is not immediately ready, perhaps because the actor isnt loaded in yet.]`


**方法**:

- `AActor ProvideActor()`  
  Provide the actor.
