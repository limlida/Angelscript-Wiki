### UGameplayTagTransitionCondition


A transition condition that matches the gameplay tags on the previous and next
camera rigs and assets. Both queries need to pass. Empty queries pass by default.

**属性**:

- `FGameplayTagQuery NextGameplayTagQuery [The gameplay tags to look for on the next camera rig/asset.]`
- `FGameplayTagQuery PreviousGameplayTagQuery [The gameplay tags to look for on the previous camera rig/asset.]`
