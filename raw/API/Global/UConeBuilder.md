### UConeBuilder


**属性**:

- `bool AlignToSide [Whether to align the brush to a face]`
- `float32 CapZ [Distance from base to the tip of inner cone (when hollow)]`
- `bool Hollow [Whether this is a hollow or solid cone]`
- `float32 InnerRadius [Radius of inner cone (when hollow)]`
- `float32 OuterRadius [Radius of cone]`
- `int Sides [How many sides this cone should have]`
- `float32 Z [Distance from base to tip of cone]`
