### UMassLookAtProcessor


Processor to choose and assign LookAt configurations

**属性**:

- `float32 AngleThresholdInDegrees [Tolerance in degrees between the forward direction and the look at duration to track an entity]`
- `float32 DebugZOffset [Height offset that will be added for debug draw of the look at vector. Useful to bring arrow near character's eyes]`
- `float32 Duration [Time an entity must use a random look at.]`
- `float32 DurationVariation [Variation applied to a random look at duration [Duration-Variation : Duration+Variation]]`
- `float32 QueryExtent [Size of the query to find potential targets]`
