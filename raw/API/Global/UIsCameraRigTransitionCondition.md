### UIsCameraRigTransitionCondition


A transition condition that matches the previous and/or next camera rigs against
one of the owning camera asset's rigs.

**属性**:

- `UCameraRigAsset NextCameraRig [Passes if null, or equal to the next camera rig.]`
- `UCameraRigAsset PreviousCameraRig [Passes if null, or equal to the previous camera rig.]`
