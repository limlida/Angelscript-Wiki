### UBTDecorator_CompareBBEntries


Blackboard comparison decorator node.
A decorator node that bases its condition on a comparison between two Blackboard keys.

**属性**:

- `FBlackboardKeySelector BlackboardKeyA [blackboard key selector]`
- `FBlackboardKeySelector BlackboardKeyB [blackboard key selector]`
- `EBlackBoardEntryComparison Operator [operation type]`
