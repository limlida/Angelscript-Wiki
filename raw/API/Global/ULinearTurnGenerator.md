### ULinearTurnGenerator


Modular mechanism for turning a Mover actor in place using linear angular velocity.

**属性**:

- `float32 HeadingRate [Maximum angular velocity of heading changes (degrees per second). AKA Yaw, AKA Z. Negative numbers will cause rotation to snap instantly to desired orientation.]`
- `float32 PitchRate [Maximum angular velocity of pitch changes (degrees per second). Negative numbers will cause rotation to snap instantly to desired orientation.]`
- `float32 RollRate [Maximum angular velocity of roll changes (degrees per second). Negative numbers will cause rotation to snap instantly to desired orientation.]`
