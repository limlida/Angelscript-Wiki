### UBakeVisualizationProperties


**属性**:

- `float32 AOMultiplier [Ambient Occlusion multiplier in the viewport; does not affect results stored in textures]`
- `float32 Brightness [Adjust the brightness of the preview material; does not affect results stored in textures]`
- `bool bPreviewAsMaterial [Preview the texture as an input to the respective material parameter (ex. Normal, Ambient Occlusion)]`
