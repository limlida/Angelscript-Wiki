### UCameraModifier_CameraShake


A UCameraModifier_CameraShake is a camera modifier that can apply a UCameraShakeBase to
the owning camera.

**属性**:

- `float32 SplitScreenShakeScale [Scaling factor applied to all camera shakes in when in splitscreen mode. Normally used to reduce shaking, since shakes feel more intense in a smaller viewport.]`
