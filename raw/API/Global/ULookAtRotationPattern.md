### ULookAtRotationPattern


Stare at a single fixed point at all times (pairs nicely with ellipse if you want to always look at the center)

**属性**:

- `FVector RelativeLookAtLocation [The location relative to the path origin to always look at while moving along the path]`


**方法**:

- `SetLookAtLocation(FVector LookAt)`
- `SetRelativeLookAtLocation(FVector RelativeLookAt)`
