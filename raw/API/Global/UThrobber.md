### UThrobber


A Throbber widget that shows several zooming circles in a row.

**属性**:

- `FSlateBrush Image [The animated pieces.]`
- `int NumberOfPieces [How many pieces there are]`
- `bool bAnimateHorizontally [Should the pieces animate horizontally?]`
- `bool bAnimateOpacity [Should the pieces animate their opacity?]`
- `bool bAnimateVertically [Should the pieces animate vertically?]`


**方法**:

- `SetAnimateHorizontally(bool bInAnimateHorizontally)`  
  Sets whether the pieces animate horizontally.
- `SetAnimateOpacity(bool bInAnimateOpacity)`  
  Sets whether the pieces animate their opacity.
- `SetAnimateVertically(bool bInAnimateVertically)`  
  Sets whether the pieces animate vertically.
- `SetNumberOfPieces(int InNumberOfPieces)`  
  Sets how many pieces there are
