### UDampenRotationCameraNode


A camera node that smoothes the rotation of the camera.

**属性**:

- `FFloatCameraParameter PitchDampingFactor [Damping factor for the camera pitch.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter RollDampingFactor [Damping factor for the camera roll.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter YawDampingFactor [Damping factor for the camera yaw.]`
