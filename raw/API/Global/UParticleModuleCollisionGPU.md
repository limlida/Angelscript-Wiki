### UParticleModuleCollisionGPU


**属性**:

- `EParticleCollisionMode CollisionMode []`
- `float32 Friction [Friction applied to all particles during a collision or while moving
along a surface.]`
- `float32 RadiusBias [Bias applied to the collision radius.]`
- `float32 RadiusScale [Scale applied to the size of the particle to obtain the collision radius.]`
- `float32 RandomDistribution [Controls bouncing particles distribution (1 = uniform distribution; 2 = squared distribution).]`
- `float32 RandomSpread [Controls how wide the bouncing particles are distributed (0 = disabled).]`
- `FRawDistributionFloat Resilience [The bounciness of the particle.]`
- `FRawDistributionFloat ResilienceScaleOverLife [Scales the bounciness of the particle over its life.]`
- `EParticleCollisionResponse Response [How particles respond to a collision event.]`
