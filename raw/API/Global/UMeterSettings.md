### UMeterSettings


UMeterSettings

Settings for a UMeterAnalyzer.

**属性**:

- `float32 AnalysisPeriod [Number of seconds between meter measurements]`
- `float32 ClippingThreshold [What volume threshold to throw clipping detection notifications.]`
- `int MeterAttackTime [Meter attack time, in milliseconds]`
- `int MeterReleaseTime [Meter release time, in milliseconds.]`
- `int PeakHoldTime [Peak hold time, in milliseconds]`
- `EMeterPeakType PeakMode [Meter envelope type type]`
