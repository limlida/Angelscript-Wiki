### UFractureSettings


Settings specifically related to viewing fractured meshes *

**属性**:

- `float32 ExplodeAmount [Amount to expand the displayed Geometry Collection bones into an 'exploded view']`
- `int FractureLevel [Current level of the Geometry Collection displayed]`
- `EFractureSelectionDisplayMode SelectionDisplayMode [How to display the selection in the viewport]`
- `bool bHideUnselected [When active, only show selected bones]`
