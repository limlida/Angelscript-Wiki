### UFilmbackCameraNode


A camera node that configures the camera filmback.

**属性**:

- `EAspectRatioAxisConstraint AspectRatioAxisConstraint [Defines the axis along which to constrain the aspect ratio of the evaluated camera.
Only used when ConstrainAspectRatio is false and OverrideAspectRatioAxisConstraint is true.]`
- `FBooleanCameraParameter ConstrainAspectRatio [Whether to constrain the aspect ratio of the evaluated camera.]`
- `FBooleanCameraParameter OverrideAspectRatioAxisConstraint [Whether to override the default aspect ratio axis constraint defined on the player controller.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter Overscan [Sensor overscan, in percentages (0% for no increase).]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter SensorHeight [Vertical size of filmback or digital sensor, in mm.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter SensorHorizontalOffset [Horizontal offset of the sensor, in mm.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter SensorVerticalOffset [Vertical offset of the sensor, in mm.]`
- `FFloatCameraParameter SensorWidth [Horizontal size of filmback or digital sensor, in mm.]`
