### UOptimusSkinWeightsAsVertexMaskDataInterface


Compute Framework Data Interface for merging skin weights of one or more bones into a per-vertex float map.

**属性**:

- `TArray<FName> BoneNames [Weights of selected bones are combined to form a single a vertex weight map]`
- `FColor DebugDrawColor []`
- `int ExpandTowardsLeaf [Include children up to the specified depth]`
- `int ExpandTowardsRoot [Include all bones within the radius by expanding selection towards the root]`
- `FName SkinWeightProfile []`
- `bool bDebugDrawIncludedBones []`
