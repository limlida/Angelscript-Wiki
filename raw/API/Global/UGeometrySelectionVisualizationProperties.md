### UGeometrySelectionVisualizationProperties


**属性**:

- `float32 DepthBias [Depth bias used to slightly shift depth of points/lines]`
- `FColor FaceColor [Color used to render the geometry selection]`
- `FColor LineColor [Color used to render the geometry selection]`
- `float32 LineThickness [Line thickness used to render the geometry selection]`
- `FColor PointColor [Color used to render the geometry selection]`
- `float32 PointSize [Point size used to render the geometry selection]`
- `FColor TriangleROIBorderColor [Color used to render the geometry selection ROI]`
- `bool bShowEdgeSelectionVertices [This tool treats edge selections as vertex selections. Enable this to show the edited vertices]`
- `bool bShowHidden [Show occluded parts of the selection]`
- `bool bShowSelection [Render the geometry selection]`
- `bool bShowTriangleROIBorder [Render the geometry selection as the border of the triangles in the ROI (region of influence)]`
