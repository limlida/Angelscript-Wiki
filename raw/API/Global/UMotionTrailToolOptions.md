- `FLinearColor DashPostColor [The color of the next motion trail color when alternating between frames]`
- `FLinearColor DashPreColor [The color of the first motion trail color when alternating between frames]`
- `FLinearColor DefaultColor [The color of the motion trail]`
- `int FramesAfter [The number of frames to draw after the end of the trail. Requires not showing the full trail]`
- `int FramesBefore [The number of frames to draw before the start of the trail. Requires not showing the full trail]`
- `FLinearColor KeyColor [The color of the keys]`
- `float KeySize [The size of the keys]`
- `FLinearColor MarkColor [The color of the marks]`
- `float MarkSize [The size of the marks]`
- `int MaxNumberPinned [Max number of pinned trails]`
- `FLinearColor SelectedKeyColor [The color of the selected keys]`
- `FLinearColor TimePostColor [The color of the motion trail after current time if show alternating time colors]`
- `FLinearColor TimePreColor [The color of the motion trail before current time if show alternating time colors]`
- `EMotionTrailTrailStyle TrailStyle [How To Show Color]`
- `float TrailThickness [The thickness of the motion trail]`
- `bool bShowFullTrail [Whether or not to show the full motion trail]`
- `bool bShowKeys [Whether or not to show keys on the motion trail]`
- `bool bShowMarks [Whether or not to show marks along the motion trail]`
- `bool bShowSelectedTrails [Whether or not to show selected motion trails]`
- `bool bShowTrails [Whether or not to show motion trails]`
