### UFractureRemoveOnBreakSettings


**属性**:

- `bool ClusterCrumbling [When set, clusters will crumble when post break timer expires, non clusters will simply use the removal timer]`
- `bool Enabled [whether or not the remove on fracture is enabled]`
- `FVector2f PostBreakTimer [Min/Max time after break before removal starts]`
- `FVector2f RemovalTimer [Min/Max time for how long removal lasts - not applicable when cluster crumbling is on]`
