### UTimelineTemplate


**属性**:

- `ETimelineLengthMode LengthMode [How we want the timeline to determine its own length (e.g. specified length, last keyframe)]`
- `TArray<FBPVariableMetaDataEntry> MetaDataArray [Metadata information for this timeline]`
- `float32 TimelineLength [Length of this timeline]`
- `bool bAutoPlay [If we want the timeline to auto-play]`
- `bool bIgnoreTimeDilation [If we want the timeline to ignore global time dilation]`
- `bool bLoop [If we want the timeline to loop]`
- `bool bReplicated [If we want the timeline to loop]`
