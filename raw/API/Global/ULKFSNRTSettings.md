### ULKFSNRTSettings


ULKFSNRTSettings

Settings for a ULKFSNRT analyzer.

**属性**:

- `float32 AnalysisPeriod [Number of seconds between loudness measurements]`
- `float32 AnalysisWindowDuration [Number of seconds of audio analyzed for a single window of measurement]`
- `float32 ShortTermLoudnessDuration [Number of seconds of audio analyzed when determining short term loudness.]`
