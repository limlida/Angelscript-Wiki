### UAttachToActorCameraNode


A camera node that moves the camera to an actor.

**属性**:

- `FBooleanCameraParameter AttachToLocation [Whether to move the camera to the actor's location.]`
- `FBooleanCameraParameter AttachToRotation [Whether to align the camera rotation to the actor's orientation.]`
- `FCameraActorAttachmentInfo Attachment [The actor to attach to.]`
