### UTileSheetPaddingFactory


Factory used to pad out each individual tile in a tile sheet texture

**属性**:

- `int ExtrusionAmount [The amount to extrude out from each tile (in pixels)]`
- `bool bFillWithTransparentBlack [Should we use transparent black or white when filling the texture areas that aren't covered by tiles?]`
- `bool bPadToPowerOf2 [Should we pad the texture to the next power of 2?]`
