### UFractureSliceSettings


**属性**:

- `float32 SliceAngleVariation [Maximum angle (in degrees) to randomly rotate each slicing plane]`
- `float32 SliceOffsetVariation [Maximum distance (in cm) to randomly shift each slicing plane]`
- `int SlicesX [Number of slices along the X axis]`
- `int SlicesY [Number of slices along the Y axis]`
- `int SlicesZ [Number of slices along the Z axis]`
