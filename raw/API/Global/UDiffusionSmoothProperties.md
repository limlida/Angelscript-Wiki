### UDiffusionSmoothProperties


Properties for Diffusion Smoothing

**属性**:

- `float32 SmoothingPerStep [Amount of smoothing allowed per step. Smaller steps will avoid things like collapse of small/thin features.]`
- `int Steps [Number of Smoothing iterations]`
- `bool bPreserveUVs [If this is false, the smoother will try to reshape the triangles to be more regular, which will distort UVs]`
