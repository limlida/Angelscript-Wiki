### USoundNodeOscillator


Defines how a sound oscillates

**属性**:

- `float32 AmplitudeMax [An amplitude of 0.25 would oscillate between 0.75 and 1.25.]`
- `float32 AmplitudeMin [An amplitude of 0.25 would oscillate between 0.75 and 1.25.]`
- `float32 CenterMax [A center of 0.5 would oscillate around 0.5.]`
- `float32 CenterMin [A center of 0.5 would oscillate around 0.5.]`
- `float32 FrequencyMax [A frequency of 20 would oscillate at 10Hz.]`
- `float32 FrequencyMin [A frequency of 20 would oscillate at 10Hz.]`
- `float32 OffsetMax [Offset into the sine wave. Value modded by 2 * PI.]`
- `float32 OffsetMin [Offset into the sine wave. Value modded by 2 * PI.]`
- `bool bModulatePitch [Whether to oscillate pitch.]`
- `bool bModulateVolume [Whether to oscillate volume.]`
